PARTICIPATING BRANDS

Established in 1972 in Al Bidda, Doha - Qatar, the Al Ansari Group has a rich history that spans over 50 years. What started as a small neighborhood store has evolved into a leading conglomerate of companies with a commitment to delivering exceptional value and customer service. We take pride in our roots as the pioneers of the supermarket industry in Qatar, and we continue to uphold our founding vision with an even wider reach and enhanced services.

Mission

At Al Ansari Group, we are driven by a simple yet powerful vision - to cater to our residents with unparalleled value and customer service. This vision has been the guiding light for our journey of growth and expansion. Today, we operate seven well-equipped Retail Mart Hypermarket outlets and our flagship New Indian Supermarket, conveniently located in Old Airport, Qatar.

Quality Assurance

We understand the importance of providing the best for our customers. With over 15,000 customers per day, we are committed to delivering freshness and quality. To achieve this, we source most of our supplies through air and sea freight, ensuring that our products are of the highest quality.

Join us on our journey as we continue to serve the people of Qatar with dedication, quality, and innovation. At Al Ansari Group, we believe that our customers are at the heart of everything we do.
